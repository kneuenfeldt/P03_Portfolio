HELLO!

My name is Kam Neuenfeldt. I'm a Wisconsin born level designer studying media arts and interactive systems in the Interactive Arts and Technology program at Simon Fraser University in Vancouver. I love being part of the ideation, planning, and implementation process of level design. My strongest skills are in programming as well as 3D modeling and web development.

My greatest passion is playing videogames, especially the Sonic the Hedgehog franchise which I have made several mods of the original Genesis Platformers. I also love being involved in sports, having played hockey and soccer competitively for over 15 years.
